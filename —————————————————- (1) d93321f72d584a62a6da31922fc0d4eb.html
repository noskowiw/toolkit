As a developer, you have the ability to make decisions at both the most granular and highest architectural level. The sustainability of your application depends on a number of factors, including:

  • the scaling strategy your solution uses,
  • the types of hardware you're running it on,
  • the algorithm or design pattern you're using, and even the programming language you're coding in. In order to make your solution as energy-efficient as possible, when making decisions that affect any one of these factors you should always consider the energy efficiency impact it would have on your product . Although these decisions may have a relatively small impact on their own, adhering to all the best practices in a solution deployed at scale can significantly reduce the overall energy consumption of your application and, consequently, the emissions it's responsible for.
