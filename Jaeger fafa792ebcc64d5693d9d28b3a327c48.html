Jaeger

Jaeger is an open source, end-to-end distributed tracing system used to monitor and troubleshoot application performance. It is designed to help developers and DevOps teams understand and optimize complex, distributed systems. Jaeger is capable of tracing transactions across multiple microservices and can be integrated with various programming languages and frameworks. It also offers a user-friendly web interface for visualizing and analyzing trace data.
