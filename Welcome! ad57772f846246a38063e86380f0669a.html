Welcome!

Welcome to the Roche Cloud Sustainability Toolkit! We are excited to offer you a comprehensive collection of tools, best practices and design patterns that will help you to make both your cloud architecture and the code you're running on it as sustainable and energy efficient as possible. By implementing the suggestions presented in this toolkit, you will be able to reduce your environmental impact and operate your cloud more responsibly.

Our toolkit is designed to cater to a wide range of users. Whether you're just starting out or already have an existing cloud architecture, we have included a variety of resources and suggestions that can be implemented at any point in the development process. You will find valuable insights and recommendations to help you streamline your cloud usage and minimize your carbon footprint specific to your role.

In addition, our toolkit provides you with a wealth of information about sustainable cloud practices. By exploring the different sections of our site, you can learn more about the benefits of sustainable cloud computing, the latest trends and developments in the field, and how you can make a positive impact on the environment.

Overall, we believe that this toolkit is an essential resource for anyone who wants to operate their cloud in a more sustainable and responsible way. We hope that you find it informative, useful, and inspiring.
